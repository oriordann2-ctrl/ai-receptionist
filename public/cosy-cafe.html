Our Story

A little café with a big heart

Founded in 2016 by Sebastien Perey, Cosy Café has been a Church Square institution ever since — a dog-friendly spot where locals and Wild Atlantic Way visitors return again and again.

Everything is made fresh in our open kitchen. Free range eggs from Beechwood Farm, fish from Haven Shellfish, vegetables from Horizon Farm — real ingredients, real cooking.

Whether you're a local regular or stopping in from the road, pull up a chair. You're always welcome here.
