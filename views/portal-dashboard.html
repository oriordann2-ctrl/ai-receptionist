Help & Tutorials

Learn how to get the most from your Sprimal AI receptionist.

Getting Started
The essentials to get your AI receptionist live.
1. Embed the chat widget on your website
Go to Receptionist → copy the one-line embed code → paste it just before the </body> tag on every page of your site. Works with Wix, WordPress, Squarespace, and custom sites.
2. Add your knowledge
Go to Knowledge → upload documents (PDFs, Word files) or let Sprimal crawl your website automatically. The more you add, the better your assistant answers.
3. Share the QR code
Download the QR code from Receptionist and print it for your noticeboard, reception desk, or window. Members scan it to chat instantly — no app needed.
Managing Your Knowledge Base
Keep your assistant accurate and up-to-date.
Uploading documents — Go to Knowledge → Knowledge Base → drag and drop PDFs or Word documents. Each document is processed and made searchable within a few seconds.
Website crawl — If you have a website, Sprimal can automatically import its content. Use the crawl option in Knowledge Base and enter your URL.
Chat Flows — Create scripted conversation paths for common queries (e.g. "Book a class", "Get directions"). Go to Automation → Chat Flows → New Flow.
Flagged answers — Review answers your assistant got wrong in Knowledge → Flagged. Add correct answers to improve future responses.
Conversations & Leads
Stay on top of who's talking to your assistant.
Recent Conversations — See every chat your assistant has had. Click any row to read the full exchange. Use this to spot gaps in your knowledge base.
Leads — When a visitor shares their contact details, they appear here as a lead. Export or follow up directly.
Unanswered Questions — Questions your assistant couldn't answer confidently. Add answers to your knowledge base so it does better next time.
Reading Your Analytics
Understand what your visitors are asking.
Conversation trends — See how many chats happen each day over the last 30 days. Spikes often correspond to promotions or events.
Top topics — The most common subjects visitors ask about. Use these to prioritise what knowledge to add next.
No-Show Report (tennis clubs) — Track court booking no-shows by day, week, month or quarter. Appears in Analytics once court check-in is enabled.
Settings & Billing
Customise your assistant and manage your subscription.
Assistant name & persona — Change what your assistant calls itself and how it greets visitors under Settings → Feature Settings.
Billing — View your current plan, next invoice date, and update your payment method under Settings → Billing.
