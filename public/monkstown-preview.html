Our Story

Where Tennis Meets the Sea

Founded in 1880 on the grounds of the historic De Vesci Estate, Monkstown LTCC is one of the oldest sports clubs in Ireland. The club sits in the heart of Monkstown village on the western shore of Cork Harbour — with Monkstown Castle rising above and the marina below.

Just 15km from Cork city, the club has been a cornerstone of the Monkstown community for over a century and a half — running competitive leagues, junior programmes, summer camps and social events while maintaining the warmth of a true village club.

Whether you're a competitive player or picking up a racket for the first time — all abilities, all ages, all welcome.
