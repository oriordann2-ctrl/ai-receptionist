About Us

A little café with a big heart

We're a dog-friendly café tucked into Church Square in Kinsale — serving up delicious breakfasts, brunches and lunches made with locally sourced ingredients.

From our free range eggs to Clonakilty pudding and freshly baked scones, everything is made with care and served with a smile.

Whether you're a local regular or exploring the Wild Atlantic Way, you're always welcome at Cosy Café.

🐾 Dogs welcome inside & out
