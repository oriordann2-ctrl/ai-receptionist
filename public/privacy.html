Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your information when you interact with a Sprimal-powered assistant.

Last updated: June 2026

01 Who we are

Sprimal is an AI receptionist platform operated by Daniel O'Riordan, based in Cork, Ireland.

Contact: hello@sprimal.com
Website: www.sprimal.com

02 What we collect

When you use a Sprimal-powered chat widget on a business website, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • The content of your chat conversation

03 Why we collect it

This information is collected solely to help the business you are contacting respond to your enquiry. We do not use it for advertising or sell it to third parties.

04 Who sees your data

Your information is shared only with the business whose website you are chatting on. It is stored securely on servers within the European Union (Supabase EU region).

05 How long we keep it

Chat data is retained for up to 12 months and then deleted.

06 Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to access, correct or delete your data at any time. To make a request, contact us at hello@sprimal.com and we will respond within 30 days.

07 Cookies & storage

We use sessionStorage and localStorage in your browser to maintain your chat session and remember your privacy consent. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.
